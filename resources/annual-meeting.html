Proxy Materials

2021 Annual Meeting of Stockholders

The annual meeting of stockholders of CrowdStrike Holdings, Inc. will be held on June 30, 2021 at 9:00 a.m. Pacific Time. The annual meeting will be a virtual meeting of stockholders, which will be conducted via live audio webcast. Stockholders who held shares of our common stock as of close of business on May 5, 2021, the record date, will be able to attend the virtual annual meeting, submit their questions and vote online during the meeting by visiting www.virtualshareholdermeeting.com/CRWD2021 and entering the control number located on their Notice of Internet Availability of Proxy Materials or their proxy card.
